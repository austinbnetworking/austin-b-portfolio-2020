Location

Santa Monica offers many tourist attractions and those who live here know how beautiful it is. Founded in 2014, Surfing Fun! Surfing School provides classes for beginning and seasoned surfers. WAVES Surfing School is accredited by the National Instructors and Surf Schools Association. Our trainers are all certified with the SSA as well as Red Cross and First Aid.

Opportunity

Classes can be group or private, and customized to individual needs. If your in the California and want to learn to surf with the best, please come join the great team at Surfing Fun! We are certified and accredited so you will reciece modern surfing and safety training. We pride ourselves in being versatile to provide a great experience to those who join. While the majority of our team is young men and women we strongly urge all groups and ages to come and join in on the fun!
