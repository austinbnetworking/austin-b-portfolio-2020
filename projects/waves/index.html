Welcome to Surfing Fun!

Great Experiences

Founded in 2014, Surfing Fun! Surfing School provides classes for beginning and seasoned surfers. WAVES Surfing School is accredited by the National Instructors and Surf Schools Association. Our trainers are all certified with the SSA as well as Red Cross and First Aid. Classes can be group or private, and customized to individual needs. If your in the California and want to learn to surf with the best, please come join the great team at Surfing Fun!

Choose Us

We are located in the beautiful area of Santa Monica, California! We are certified and accredited so you will reciece modern surfing and safety training. We pride ourselves in being versatile to provide a great experience to those who join. Use this website to find any information you need about our services and location. While the majority of our team is young men and women we strongly urge all groups and ages to come and join in on the fun!
